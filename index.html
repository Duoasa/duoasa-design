More about me

Interests

Outside of work, I treat my interests as an entry point for observing experiences. Details found in games, photography, music, and urban spaces often, in turn, influence how I judge rhythm, narrative, and emotion. I am drawn to moments that leave a lasting memory: a passage of light, a composition, an interaction response, or a pause that arrives at exactly the right time can all become sources of inspiration for later design work.

My story

About

UI/UX designer with a degree in Computer Science and Technology, bringing 9+ years of cross-industry experience across aviation logistics systems, financial platforms, AI products, VI systems, and brand launch materials.

I specialize in connecting rational product logic with expressive visual systems. My work spans early product definition, product roadmapping, interaction flows, interface design, VI systems, motion prototypes, and launch-ready visual assets.

I am able to bring a product from initial inspiration through to every final pixel, with hands-on experience delivering financial platforms, AI products, project websites, investor decks, and event presentation materials.

I adapt well to different types of teams, bringing highly efficient learning ability, technical understanding from my computer science background, cross-disciplinary coordination across VI, UI, UX, and front-end development, and a strong commitment to polished delivery.
